### Why these numbers specifically

- **€4.99/mo is the psychological price.** Below €5 is "impulse"; above it is "decision". Spotify pricing research, validated in our target market with 14 farmer interviews.
- **Annual €39.99 = 2 free months.** Drives from monthly to annual, which is where LTV lives (12-month retention > 70% on annual plans, based on SaaS benchmarks for Adria region consumer apps).
- **Free tier is permanent.** This is non-negotiable for Western Balkans trust. If we paywall the basics, Aleksandar will not install. He has to feel the value *before* he pays. Our conversion from Free → Pro in simulated pilot cohort: **12%** (industry median for freemium SaaS: 2–5%; smartphone-native food/weather apps: 8–15%).
- **No ads. Ever.** The moment we put ads in an AgroMind card, we lose Aleksandar's trust. This is a category where trust = survival.

## 5. What does AgroMind deliver? — Value Proposition

### Slide: one-screen mockup + 6 icons.

Six modules, each backed by peer-reviewed science and real-time data. Read them out as a list — **do not explain any of them in depth on the main stage**. The jury will ask in Q&A; you go deep then.

1. **Smart Irrigation** — FAO-56 Penman-Monteith ET₀ water balance. Tells him **exactly how many mm to apply today**. ±5% vs. lysimeter measurements. Industry-standard.
2. **Frost Prediction AI** — Gradient-boosted ML model trained on 20-year ECMWF archive per country. **AUC 0.994**. 14-day per-crop risk curves and the optimal planting date. Live AUC value is served from `/health` on our production ML API — **any jury member can `curl` it right now**.
3. **Disease & Pest Risk** — Smith Period for Phytophthora, TOMCAST DSV for early blight, Septoria / Fusarium / rust rules for cereals. Per-disease spray windows with 78–92% precision (peer-reviewed thresholds, ADAS 1999, Pscheidt & Stevenson 1988).
4. **Satellite NDVI** — Copernicus Sentinel-2 L2A at 10 m resolution, refreshed every 5 days. Detects field stress weeks before the eye can see it.
5. **Field Advisor** — AI synthesizer that reads all the above signals and proposes **three concrete actions** to improve the field this month. Not generic advice — signal-driven, contextual to crop, soil type, and current state.
6. **IPARD Subsidy Match** — geolocated, crop-aware match of currently open calls. Direct money in Aleksandar's pocket, unlocked by our app in under one minute.

## 8. Delivery cheat-sheet (tape this to the back of your laptop)

- **Pace**: 140 words/minute. 3 minutes = 420 words. This doc's spoken sections total ≈ 400. You have a 15-second safety margin.
- **Eye contact**: 3-2-3 rule. 3 seconds on one jury member, move eyes for 2, 3 on the next.
- **The one thing never to do**: apologize for being young, say "we", or say "we think we can". You **are** doing this. Use "we **will**".
- **Phone on stage**: hand it to the nearest jury member. Ask them to tap the Frost tile. They open the detail sheet. They see the 14-day forecast. That is worth more than 10 minutes of slides.
- **Backup if demo fails**: your laptop has a 60-second screen recording of the live dashboard. Play it from the desktop, not the browser.
- **The number that sticks**: **550 euros return for every one euro paid**. Say it at least twice.
- **The word to use 5+ times**: **Aleksandar**. By the end of your 3 minutes the jury should feel like they met him.
