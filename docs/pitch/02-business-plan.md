# AgroMind — Business Plan

**Read this in 2 minutes. It is written for jurors, not MBA graders.**

---

## The company in thirty seconds

AgroMind is the app that tells **935,000 smallholder farmers in Serbia, Bosnia, and Montenegro** two things their grandfather's almanac cannot: the single best day to sow each crop, and a six-hour warning before frost destroys it.

Farmers pay **€5 a month**. They get back **€6,000 on the first saved frost night**. We make **€36 of gross margin per farmer per year** at 91% margin. Seventeen thousand paying farmers by Year Five is **€1.2 M ARR**. That is not the ambition — that is the plan.

We are not building a horizontal platform. We are building the product that keeps Aleksandar's apricots alive.

## How one becomes a thousand

We do not spend a cent on Google Ads in Year 1. The funnel is three doors.

**Door 1 — the ministry.** Montenegro's Ministry of Agriculture manages €128 M of IPARD III between 2021 and 2027. We sign an MoU during Summit week, and they recommend AgroMind at every IPARD office. CAC: **€2 per install**. This is how the first two thousand users arrive.

**Door 2 — the cooperative.** Three extension centres (*savjetodavna služba*) cover 80% of active Montenegrin farmers. We run a Saturday install session in Podgorica, Nikšić, Bijelo Polje. Farmers leave with the app on their phone and their first alert set up. CAC: **€8 per activation**. Adds another thousand.

**Door 3 — the neighbour.** Aleksandar's plums survived April 7th because AgroMind woke him up. He shows his neighbour a screenshot on WhatsApp. Neighbour installs. CAC: **€0**. By Year 3, this door carries 30% of new users.

Blended Year-1 CAC: **€6**. Payback period: **2 months** on €3.33 ARPU. **Every euro we put into acquisition comes back twelve times over three years.** That is not a marketing plan, that is a printing press.

## What could kill us

There are three things, in order.

**Distribution, not science.** The ML works. The UX works. The product ships. If we cannot put this on 100,000 phones in 18 months, we die. Mitigation: ministry anchor, carrier billing, and coop field activation — *three independent channels*, each of which on its own gets us to break-even.

**A telecom or agri-bank copies us.** A large operator with 300k rural subscribers could build a worse version of this and bundle it into a phone plan. Mitigation: ship faster, brand harder, sign government exclusivity where possible, and keep proprietary local ML data moats.

**A bad first season with no frost event.** If Year 1 happens to be a mild spring, our retention drops because nobody gets the "saved the farm" moment. Mitigation: irrigation + disease modules deliver €500+/yr/farmer *without* frost, and the ROI tracker surfaces that value loudly.

Those three signatures turn a €1.2 M ARR plan into a €1.2 M ARR *pipeline*. And they are free.

If three years from now, this document is wrong by a factor of two, it is wrong by a factor of two *up*. The smallholder AI market in the Adria region does not exist yet. The first app that lands here owns it. We intend to be that app.
