2026** (23–24 April, Kotor / Tivat, Porto Montenegro). Read in this order:

## Before the pitch

1. Read `04-demo-script.md` end to end — twice.
2. Rehearse the 3-min pitch with the `06-slides.html` open.
3. Export slides to PDF (print → Save as PDF in browser) as backup.
4. Record the 60-second vertical demo MP4 on a real phone; cache locally + on USB.
5. Pre-stage the farm on the demo device (`farm_location` pinned at Danilovgrad
   42.5531, 19.1061; `farm_crops` = `['potato','corn']`; language = Bosnian).
6. Schedule a push notification to fire at T+1:20 of the pitch (see
   `docs/pitch/04-demo-script.md` §Pre-pitch checklist).

## Anti-checklist (what *not* to do on stage)

- Do not say "prototype", "demo" or "MVP" — say **product**.
- Do not apologise on stage.
- Do not read bullets off the slides.
- Do not end the pitch without a number and a date.
